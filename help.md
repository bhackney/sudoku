

# Suduko

There 2 major help modes (cheats) for this Sudoku game.

First, Full Grid highlighting makes seeing the eligible
empty squares very easy for beginners. Click on any filled
in number, and all rows and columns that have that number
are highlighted, leaving the other ones easy to spot.

Second, a real cheat is to automatically fill in the hints.

The Check button finds errors in the notes.

The Hint button reveals a number - not very creative.

When in Notes or Full Grid mode, pressing the Shift key
allows number entry - not note entry. This avoids having
to click over to Normal mode and then back.

Bookmark allows you to go up to 3 steps forward, and then
restore back to the bookmark.
