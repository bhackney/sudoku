SUDOKU — HELP

There are 2 major help modes (cheats) for this Sudoku game.

Full Grid highlighting makes seeing the eligible empty squares very easy for beginners. Click on any filled-in number, and all rows and columns that have that number are highlighted, leaving the other ones easy to spot.

Fill Notes is a real cheat — it automatically fills in all valid candidates as notes.

Check finds errors in the notes — candidates that conflict with already-placed numbers.

Hint reveals a single number.

When in Notes or Full Grid mode, holding Shift while pressing a number enters a guess instead of a note, without having to switch modes.

Bookmark saves your current position. You can then make up to 3 more moves and use Restore to return to it. Mistakes are restored; notes are not.
